About Us

ResuMaker was born when a group of students decided that resumes should not require endless fiddling with formats and layouts. We hope that ResuMaker provides a simpler, and yet more powerful approach to resumes.

We are, CS2103-W-17-1, a team based in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore.
